Chimamanda Ngozi Adiche Novels

Ever since I opened Half of a Yellow Sun, I fell in love with novels by the author.

Here are some of the reasons I love Chimamanda Novels:
  • Strong female Leads
  • No stereotypical fairytale endings
  • Tackles taboo's amongst members of the African Diaspora
Here is a ranked list of Chimamanda's Novels:
  1. Purple Hibiscus
  2. Half of a Yellow Sun
  3. The Thing Around your Neck
